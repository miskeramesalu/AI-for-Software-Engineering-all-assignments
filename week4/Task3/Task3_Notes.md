Predictive analytics leverages AI and machine learning to anticipate outcomes and guide decisions in software projects. AI-assisted predictive modeling enables data-driven development by forecasting project quality and performance trends. However, ethical validation and explainability remain vital to ensure trustworthy use. The integration of predictive analytics into DevOps pipelines exemplifies how AI can continuously enhance quality assurance, productivity, and decision support in software engineering.
