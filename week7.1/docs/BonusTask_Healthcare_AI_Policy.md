# Ethical AI Policy for Healthcare

## 1. Patient Consent Protocols
- Explicit consent before using patient data  
- Right to withdraw data at any time  
- Clear explanation of AI purpose  

## 2. Bias Mitigation Strategies
- Use diverse medical datasets  
- Check performance across age, gender, ethnicity  
- Regular fairness audits  

## 3. Transparency Requirements
- Explainable AI models  
- Public documentation  
- Human review before medical decisions
